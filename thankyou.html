Prepare for Your Call

How to Get the Most From Your Strategy Call

Know Your Goals

Before the call, think about what matters most to you: passive income, tax benefits, diversification, or long-term wealth building. Knowing your priorities helps us point you to the right opportunities.

Know Your Numbers

Have an idea of how much you are looking to invest ($10,000 minimum) and your timeline. If you have current portfolio details or tax information, have them nearby.

Bring Your Questions

About our process, our track record, specific deals, the risks, the tax benefits, anything. There are no bad questions. We will give you straight answers.

Who This Is and Is Not For

This call is for you if:

  • You are an accredited investor (net worth $1M+ or income $200K+)
  • You have at least $10,000 available to invest
  • You are looking for passive real estate income without managing properties
  • You understand that syndication investments are illiquid (3-7 year holds)
  • You are comfortable with the inherent risks of real estate investing

This call is not for you if:

  • You need access to your capital within the next 12 months
  • You are seeking guaranteed returns (no legitimate operator can guarantee returns)
  • You are not an accredited investor
  • You are not willing to invest at least $10,000
  • You are looking for day-trading or short-term speculation

If any of the "not for you" criteria apply, please reschedule. We would rather save both of our time and connect when the timing is right.
