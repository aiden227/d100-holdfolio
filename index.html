The Co-Investment Model

How It Works

Three steps. Fully passive. Your money works alongside ours.

1

We Source and Vet

We review hundreds of deals every year across multifamily, storage, NNN, and commercial. We partner with experienced operators across 19 states. Out of hundreds, we select roughly one per month.

2

We Invest First

Before opening any deal to investors, Holdfolio invests its own capital. We put our money in first. Same deal, same terms, same alignment. If we would not invest our own capital, we do not ask you to.

3

You Co-Invest and Earn

You invest starting at $10,000. You receive quarterly distributions. You benefit from an 80%+ tax shield through depreciation. You never manage a single property. One-time 2.5% fee. No annual fees.

Frequently Asked Questions

Minimums start at $10,000-$20,000 depending on the deal. This is significantly lower than most syndications, which typically require $50,000-$100,000.

Holdfolio investments are available to accredited investors. An accredited investor has a net worth exceeding $1 million (excluding primary residence) or annual income exceeding $200,000 ($300,000 joint) for the past two years.

We charge a one-time 2.5% fee at the time of investment. No ongoing annual management fees. We also earn a share of the profits alongside our investors, which aligns our incentives.

Our historical average across 155 full-cycle deals is 17.9% annualized. Current deals target 15-22% annualized returns. Past performance is not indicative of future results. Target returns are projections, not guarantees.

Hold periods are typically 3-7 years depending on the deal. These are illiquid investments with no secondary market. Only invest capital you do not need in the short term.

Investors typically benefit from an 80%+ tax shield through depreciation and paper losses that offset ordinary income. Specific tax benefits depend on your situation. Consult your tax advisor.

Real estate investments carry risk, including loss of principal. We mitigate risk through operator vetting, diversification across 19 states and 7 asset types, and co-investing our own capital. If a deal underperforms, we lose money alongside you.

Holdfolio is a private real estate investment platform that co-invests alongside accredited investors in institutional-quality real estate deals across the United States.
